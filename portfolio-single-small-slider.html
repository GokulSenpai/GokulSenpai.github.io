GRAYDIUS

2D Platformer

Overview

GRAYDIUS is a fast-paced Metroidvania inspired by Dandara and Darksiders and Dark Souls.

It is 2D Platformer with the elements of RPG Exploration and Puzzle-Solving.

Every boss, every collectible, every location has a story to tell.

The player takes on the role of “GRAY” who must battle her way out of enemies using the power of the gauntlet.
